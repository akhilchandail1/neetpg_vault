Ortolani vs BarlowOrtolani = REDUCES dislocation; Barlow = DISLOCATES hip
Intussusception vs Volvulus ageIntussusception 6–18 months; Volvulus — any age (malrotation neonates)
PSGN vs IgA nephropathy latencyPSGN: 1–3 wks; IgA: 1–3 DAYS (synpharyngitic)
Croup vs EpiglottitisCroup = Steeple sign + Parainfluenza; Epiglottitis = Thumb sign + H. flu
Nephrotic vs Nephritic C3Nephrotic: C3 normal; Nephritic PSGN: C3 LOW
Hirschsprung vs Functional constipationHirschsprung: delay in meconium, squirt sign; Functional: older child
West syndrome vs Lennox-GastautWest: infantile spasms + hypsarrhythmia; LGS: multiple types + slow spike-wave
Haemophilia A vs vWDHaem A: APTT only; vWD: PT + APTT + bleeding time
Phototherapy vs Exchange transfusionPhototherapy at 15 mg/dL; Exchange at 20 mg/dL (term neonate)

📐 Michaelis-Menten Kinetics
v = Vmax × [S] / (Km + [S])
Km definition[S] at ½Vmax = inverse measure of affinity
Low KmHigh affinity
Competitive inhibition↑ Km; Vmax unchanged; overcome by ↑[S]
Non-competitive inhibition↓ Vmax; Km unchanged; cannot overcome
Uncompetitive inhibition↓ Vmax AND ↓ Km (both decrease)
Mixed inhibition↓ Vmax; Km may increase or decrease
Allosteric enzymesSigmoidal O2 dissociation curve (cooperative)
Lineweaver-Burk1/v vs 1/[S]; x-intercept = -1/Km

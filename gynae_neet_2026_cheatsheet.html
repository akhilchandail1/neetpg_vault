💊 Combined OCP (COCP)
MechanismInhibits ovulation (LH/FSH suppression)
Pearl Index0.1–0.3 (most effective)
Absolute CIMigraine with aura, VTE, smoker >35 yrs, liver disease, breast Ca, CVD
Non-contraceptive benefits↓Dysmenorrhoea, ↓PID, ↓Ovarian Ca, ↓Endometrial Ca, ↓Fibroids
Increased riskDVT, stroke, hypertension, breast Ca (slight), cervical Ca
Start timingDay 1–5 of cycle (immediate protection)
Missed pill rule<12 hrs: take ASAP; >12 hrs: barrier method
ACHES danger signs: Abdominal pain, Chest pain, Headache, Eye changes, Swelling of legs
🔵 Progesterone-Only Pill (POP)
MechanismThickens cervical mucus; inconsistently inhibits ovulation
TimingMust take within 3-hr window daily
Best forBreastfeeding, COCP CI patients
Side effectsIrregular bleeding, functional ovarian cysts
Desogestrel POP12-hr window; more effective

Sterilization & Disinfection
Methods, agents, sporicidal activity, hospital infection control
🧪 Definitions & Hierarchy
SterilizationDestruction of ALL forms including spores
DisinfectionKills pathogens but NOT all spores
AntisepticUsed on living tissue
BacteriostaticInhibits growth; reversible
BactericidalKills bacteria; irreversible
Spaulding classificationCritical → Semi-critical → Non-critical
Critical itemsEnter sterile tissue → STERILIZATION required (surgical instruments)
Semi-criticalContact mucous membrane → High-level disinfection (endoscopes)
Non-criticalContact intact skin → Low-level disinfection (BP cuffs)
